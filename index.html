WHO IS
MOROSS

Aries Moross is a renowned graphic designer and art director known for their vibrant and innovative approach to design. Based in London, Aries has made a significant impact in the music and entertainment industries, creating visually striking album covers, branding, and promotional materials. Their work blends bold colors and playful typography, reflecting a deep passion for creativity and a unique aesthetic vision. With a diverse portfolio that includes collaborations with major artists and brands, Aries continues to push the boundaries of contemporary design.
