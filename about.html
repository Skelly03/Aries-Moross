THE CURTAIN

Aries Moross is a creative director, illustrator and designer. Moross began their career in 2007, illustrating hand drawn flyers for London's independent music scene, and in 2008, established a vinyl only record label

Over the course of their career, they've maintained close ties to the music industry whilst expanding into the realm of both art and creative direction

Moross has taken on the role as creative director for a range of clients from pop artists to DJs, with projects spanning everything from album campaigns to live shows.

Notable clients include Kylie Minogue, H.E.R, Disclosure, Spice Girls, Jessie Ware, and London Grammar
